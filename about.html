Research Interests

🤖 Large Language Models

Understanding how to scale, optimize, and deploy LLMs effectively. Interested in efficient inference, fine-tuning strategies, and prompt engineering.

🏗️ ML Systems & Infrastructure

Building the systems and tools that enable ML research and production. Distributed training, data pipelines, and MLOps are key areas of focus.

⚡ Model Optimization

Making models faster, smaller, and more efficient through quantization, pruning, distillation, and other optimization techniques.

🔍 Natural Language Processing

Advancing the state-of-the-art in understanding and generating human language. From embeddings to sequence models, NLP is fascinating.

👁️ Computer Vision

Building models that can see and understand visual information. Vision transformers, efficient detection, and multi-modal learning.

📊 Interpretability & Safety

Understanding what models learn and ensuring they behave reliably. Critical for deploying AI responsibly.
